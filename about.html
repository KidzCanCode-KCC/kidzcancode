Hi! I'm Morola Omotoso — a 15-year-old coder, teacher, and Jesus-loving teen who's passionate about helping kids unlock the power of code! 🙌✨

🚀 Why KidzCanCode?

We believe every kid deserves a chance to explore tech in a safe, fun, and faith-filled space! Whether you're a total beginner or ready to build games, we're here to help. 🎉💻

📚 What We Use

  • 💬 Zoom for lessons
  • 🧩 Scratch for beginners
  • 🐍 Python for logic and games
  • 🌐 HTML/CSS & JavaScript for websites
  • 🎮 Roblox Lua for 3D game creation (group only)

🙏 Our Mission

To empower kids to build the future, one line of code at a time — with joy, creativity, and Christ at the center. 🕊️
